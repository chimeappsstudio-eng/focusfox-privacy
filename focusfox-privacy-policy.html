Privacy Policy for FocusFox

Last updated: July 23, 2026

FocusFox ("the App", "we", "us") is a focus and screen-time app for Android built by an independent developer. This policy explains what data the App accesses, why, and how it is handled. It applies to FocusFox and its localized version (沸沸 / FocusFoxCN).

1. Data we access

FocusFox needs certain Android system permissions to function. Here is what each one is used for:

  • Usage Access (UsageStatsManager): to measure how long you use other apps, so FocusFox can show you usage reports and enforce the focus limits you set.
  • Notification Access (NotificationListenerService): to detect and block notifications from distracting apps during a focus session, and to allow notifications from contacts you have designated as "VIP" to pass through even while other apps are blocked.
  • Accessibility Service: to detect when a blocked app is opened and show the focus-lock screen over it.

FocusFox does not read the content of your messages or notifications for any purpose other than the VIP passthrough feature described above, and does not use this data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the App's focus features.

2. Where your data is stored

All data collected by FocusFox — usage statistics, your app-blocking settings, VIP contact list, and focus session history — is stored locally on your device only. FocusFox does not operate a backend server and does not transmit this data off your device.

3. Subscriptions and payments

If you purchase a FocusFox subscription, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play Billing. FocusFox does not receive or store your payment details; this is handled under Google's own privacy policy (https://policies.google.com/privacy).

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs at this time.

5. Data retention and deletion

Data stored by FocusFox is removed when you clear the app's data or uninstall the app. Note that Android's device backup feature may restore this data if you reinstall the app on the same device using the same Google account; you can turn this off in your device's backup settings if you prefer a clean reinstall.

6. Children's privacy

FocusFox is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect data from children under the age required by applicable local law to consent to use of such services.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the App changes. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.
